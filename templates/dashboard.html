💰 EVMS — Cost & Schedule Performance

EV above AC = under budget. EV below AC = over budget. PV is the plan. The gap widens when scope creep hits.

🔥 Release Burndown

Orange line = remaining scope. Upward jumps are scope creep events. Bars show per-period velocity. Ideal line assumes constant pace from day one.

💎 Scope Coverage Ratio — Budget vs. Scope Growth

Each bar is the estimated cost per point at the current budget — it steps down every time scope is added without funding. The teal line is what each point actually costs. When the line rises above a bar, that period is in overrun. Team velocity is grounded in past performance and cannot be willed higher. Adding scope without funding means the same dollars must cover more work — the result is a cost overrun, a schedule slip, or both.

📈 Performance Indices — CPI & SPI

Both indices target ≥ 1.0. A sustained CPI below the line means the project will overrun at completion — the EAC card above tells you by how much. A sustained SPI below the line means the team is delivering slower than planned and the end date is at risk.
